The Bushwick II plan was adopted in 1979, last revised in 1999, and expires in 2019. It includes residential, commercial, and park uses for lots in the plan area.

[Housing Preservation and Development, Bushwick II Sixth Amended Urban Renewal Plan (2003).](https://www.nyc.gov/assets/hpd/downloads/pdfs/services/bushwick-ii-sixth-amended-urp.pdf)
